SS-31 (Elamipretide)

What It Is & How It Works

SS-31 is a synthetic tetrapeptide that targets mitochondria, binding to cardiolipin to stabilize the inner mitochondrial membrane, enhance ATP production, and reduce oxidative stress.

Main Benefits

  • Mitochondrial protection: Reduces oxidative damage.
  • Energy production: Enhances ATP synthesis.
  • Cardioprotection: Improves heart function.
  • Neuroprotection: Supports brain health.
  • Anti-aging: Delays mitochondrial decline.

Clinical Studies

Szeto et al. (2014): Reduced ischemia/reperfusion injury (PubMed).
Phase II trials (2020): Improved cardiac function (ClinicalTrials.gov).

Dosage

4 mg/day subcutaneous (SubQ) for 20 days.

What It Is & How It Works

A GHRH analog that stimulates pituitary gland to release growth hormone, promoting muscle growth and fat loss.

Tesamorelin

What It Is & How It Works

A GHRH analog that increases GH to reduce visceral fat, particularly in HIV-associated lipodystrophy.

Clinical Studies
