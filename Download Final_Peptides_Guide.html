Recovery

Peptides in this category promote tissue repair, reduce inflammation, and accelerate recovery from injuries or intense physical activity.

BPC-157

What It Is & How It Works

BPC-157 (Body Protective Compound-157) is a synthetic peptide derived from human gastric juice. It promotes angiogenesis, collagen production, and reduces inflammation via VEGF and TGF-β pathways, aiding healing in muscles, tendons, ligaments, and the gut.

Benefits

  • Tissue Repair: Accelerates healing of muscles, tendons, and ligaments.
  • Anti-Inflammatory: Reduces inflammation for faster recovery.
  • Gut Health: Repairs gut lining, beneficial for IBS or leaky gut.
  • Neuroprotection: Supports brain tissue repair.

Clinical Studies

Animal studies show accelerated tendon/muscle healing (PMID: 23782149). Preclinical data support gut repair (PMID: 22950504). Anecdotal human reports suggest efficacy.

Dosage

250–500 mcg/day subcutaneous (SubQ) or oral, cycled 4–8 weeks, post-workout or on empty stomach.

Side Effects

Rare; mild injection site reactions or nausea.

Long-Term Safety

Limited human data; animal studies suggest safety. Monitor with medical supervision.

Legal Status

Research-only, not FDA-approved. Available via prescription.

Applications

  • Injury recovery
  • Gut health
  • Anti-inflammatory therapy
  • Neuroprotection

Muscle Growth

These peptides stimulate muscle protein synthesis, growth hormone release, and fat loss, ideal for athletes and combating sarcopenia.

CJC-1295

What It Is & How It Works

CJC-1295 is a GHRH analog stimulating pituitary HGH release, increasing IGF-1 for muscle growth and fat metabolism. Often paired with Ipamorelin.

Benefits

  • Muscle Growth: Enhances protein synthesis.
  • Fat Loss: Boosts metabolism.
  • Recovery: Speeds muscle repair.
  • Anti-Aging: Improves vitality via HGH.

Clinical Studies

Increases HGH/IGF-1 in humans (PMID: 16822960). Enhances muscle growth with Ipamorelin (PMID: 23991398).

Dosage

100–300 mcg/day SubQ, at night. Cycle 8–12 weeks.

Side Effects

Mild; water retention, joint pain, injection reactions.

Long-Term Safety

Limited data; monitor HGH levels.

Legal Status

Research-only, prescription required.

Applications

  • Muscle building
  • Fat loss
  • Recovery
  • Anti-aging

Semax

What It Is & How It Works

Semax, derived from ACTH, increases BDNF and NGF, enhancing neuroplasticity and synaptic function. It modulates dopamine/serotonin for cognition and mood. Administered nasally or SubQ.

Benefits

  • Memory & Focus: Enhances retention and attention.
  • Mood Regulation: Reduces anxiety, improves mood.
  • Neuroprotection: Protects neurons from stress.
  • Learning: Boosts cognitive performance.

Clinical Studies

Approved in Russia for stroke/cognition. Improves memory/attention (PMID: 16903981). Neurogenesis in animals (PMID: 28757776).

Dosage

100–600 mcg/day intranasal or SubQ, morning. Cycle 2–4 weeks.

Side Effects

Rare; nasal irritation, headache. Favorable safety profile.

Long-Term Safety

Well-tolerated in Russia; limited Western data.

Legal Status

Research-only outside Russia.

Applications

  • Cognitive enhancement
  • Anxiety reduction
  • Neuroprotection
  • Stroke recovery

SS-31

What It Is & How It Works

SS-31 (Elamipretide) targets mitochondria, binding cardiolipin to stabilize membranes, enhancing ATP production and reducing ROS.

Benefits

  • Energy Enhancement: Boosts ATP for cellular energy.
  • Anti-Aging: Reduces oxidative damage.
  • Neuroprotection: Protects brain cells.
  • Cardioprotection: Improves heart function.

Clinical Studies

Phase II trials show cardiac improvement (NCT02814097). Reduces ischemia/reperfusion injury (PMID: 24801158).

Dosage

10–20 mg/day SubQ, morning, empty stomach. Cycle 4–8 weeks.

Side Effects

Rare; potential allergic reactions.

Long-Term Safety

Legal Status

Research-only, not FDA-approved.
